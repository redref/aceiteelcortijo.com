Manufacturation process

- All our olives pass through a tunnel equipped with a fan, in order to remove all the leafs and get the olives clean.

 

 

-Once that the olives are clean, they fall on a scale, for been weighted and calculate the production’s performance.

-They are transported on a conveyor belt, to get to the grinding mill.

 
 

 

-After been grinded, the olives become in a wine colour paste, that has to be settled for a few hours.

 

 

- The paste is inserted inside some carcasses, which are placed ones on top of anothers, making a complet column for the press, and all is put on a hydraulic press , were the olives are slowly pressed , so that the oil start to appear.

- This oil falls inside some tanks, where some water is added, so that the oil gets free of every pollutant, and then, the oil is passed into some decanters, where the oil has to settle.
